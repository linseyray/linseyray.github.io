Linsey Raymaekers is a programmer, game developer and speaker focused on emotional storytelling and artistic expression in video games.

Based in Berlin since 2017, she works part-time as a games programmer at Honig Studios and has recently taken the plunge into the freelance world.

In her personal work she likes to explore game development as a highly personal and artistic outlet. Her work is informed by her studies in Human-Computer Interaction, Game Studies, and her experiences in poetry and music as forms of self-expression.

In general, she loves creating as well as engaging with digital media that is soft, caring, and wholesome.
